About Us

Study Selector started as a group project by Piya Chand, Sandeep, Shubham, and Tanisha in 2025. Our mission is to create a platform where students can connect, learn, and grow together. We are a student-focused website designed to make education more interactive and accessible. Through Study Selector, college students can help other students by offering tutorials, guidance, and study sessions-both online and offline. Those who need help can choose a mentor and learn by paying a small fee, making it a win-win for both learners and tutors. At its core, Study Selector is built for interaction. We believe learning becomes easier when students support each other. Whether you're looking to understand a tough subject or want to earn by teaching what you know, this is your space.
